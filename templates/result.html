Pardon My French: A French to English Poem Translator

with Semantic, Syntactic, and Emotional Similarity Scores

This French to English poem translator takes in a French poem and your English translation of the poem, translates the French poem into English using DeepL, OpenAI, and Google Translate, and calculates the semantic, syntactic, and emotional similarity between the French poem and all four of these translations.

Note that similarity calculations may take a few minutes.
